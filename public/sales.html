Safety First

Ride Safe. Ride Certified.

Online bargain bikes skip safety testing to cut costs. At JBird Bikes, we don't play with your safety. Every bike we sell is UL Certified — meaning the battery, motor, charger, and electrical system have passed rigorous independent testing.

UL (Underwriters Laboratories) certification is the gold standard of electrical safety. You can plug a UL-certified eBike into your home with absolute peace of mind.

🔥 Thermal Protection
Advanced circuitry prevents the battery from overheating during use or charging.
⚡ Overcharge Prevention
Power automatically cuts off when the battery is full — no shorts, no degradation.
🛡️ Durability Tested
Batteries tested for impact, vibration, and environmental stress to handle real-world riding.
✅ How to Verify
Look for the UL Holographic Label on the battery casing and charger of every bike we sell.
