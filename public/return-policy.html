Return & Exchange Policy

We stand behind every bike we sell. Here's exactly how we handle returns, exchanges, and warranty claims.

Last updated: May 2026  ·  Questions? Call or text (504) 521-6997

🚲

New Bike Returns & Exchanges

All new bikes sold at JBird Bikes include our exclusive JBird Pro-Build — professional assembly, no-flat tire sealant, and a 30-day full labor warranty. We want you to love your bike.

📅 30-day return window on all new bikes purchased in-store, unused and in original condition.
  • Returns accepted within 30 days of purchase date
  • Bike must be unused, unridden, and in original condition
  • Original receipt or proof of purchase required
  • Full refund or exchange at customer's choice
  • Exchange for a different model available within 30 days
  • Ridden or used bikes are not eligible for return — see warranty below
  • Bikes with damage caused by rider are not eligible for return
  • Bikes purchased through third-party retailers are not eligible

Used Bike Policy

Used bikes at JBird Bikes start at $150 and are inspected and serviced before sale. All used bikes include a 3-month JBird warranty covering defects in the bike as sold.

  • All used bikes inspected and serviced before sale
  • 3-month JBird warranty included on all used bikes
  • Exchange for store credit available within 14 days if a defect is found
  • Used bikes are sold as-is beyond the 3-month warranty period
  • Cash refunds not available on used bike sales
🔧

Repair & Service Policy

All repairs begin with a free diagnostic. We provide a clear quote before any work begins — no surprises, no hidden fees.

  • Free diagnostic on all bikes — any brand, any condition
  • Written quote provided before work begins
  • 30-day labor guarantee on all repair work performed
  • If the same issue recurs within 30 days, we fix it free
  • Parts carry manufacturer warranty where applicable
⚠️ Important: Customers are required to sign a waiver releasing the shop from responsibility for repair issues not included in the original diagnostic. Any additional issues found during repair will be quoted separately before work proceeds.

All returns and warranty claims are handled in-store. We do not process returns or warranty claims by mail at this time.
