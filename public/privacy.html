Privacy & Terms

1. Privacy Policy

At JBird Bikes, we value your privacy as much as we value precision. We only collect information you voluntarily provide (like your name or phone number) to facilitate repairs or test-rides. We never sell your data to third parties.

2. Service & Repairs

All bikes brought in for service receive a free diagnostic. Labor is warranted for 30 days. JBird Bikes is not responsible for bikes left longer than 30 days after repair completion.

3. Sales & Returns

New bikes include professional assembly and a 25-point safety check. Due to the custom nature of our "Pro-Builds," all sales are final once the bike leaves the shop, but we stand by our work with local technical support.
