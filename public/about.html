About JBird Bikes

Located directly on the Lafitte Greenway, JBird Bikes is New Orleans’ premier destination for precision eBike service and affordable, high-quality sales.

We believe that eBikes are the future of NOLA transportation. Our mission is simple: to provide the community with expert "surgical" maintenance and curated bike options that can handle our unique streets without breaking the bank.

Why "JBird"?

It stands for precision and freedom. Whether we are assembling a brand new bike from a box or diagnosing a complex electrical issue, we treat every bike with the same level of mechanical rigor.
